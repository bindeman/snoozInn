Directions

From Portland:
  1. Merge onto I-5 S
  2. Take exit 283 toward Wilsonville
  3. Use the left 2 lanes to turn left onto SW Wilsonville Rd
  4. Turn right onto Town Center Loop W
  5. Turn right at the 1st cross street onto SW Main St
  6. Turn left onto SW Parkway Ave
