Local Events

Special events and festivals are occur every season in the Portland/Woodburn area. Make sure to stop by for the next festival!

Portland Rose Festival: June

The Portland Rose Festival, Oregon’s premier civic celebration, has been a Northwest tradition for over 100 years. This unique festival bursts into bloom each spring to celebrate the City of Roses with events, excitement and entertainment for all ages. More than two million spectators are in the know. Now here’s your chance to get in on the fun! With the theme Completely Captivating, Portland Rose Festival kicks off June.

rosefestival.org

Waterfront Blues Festival: July

The annual Safeway Waterfront Blues Festival not only attracts top blues artists from throughout the world, it is also a major fund-raiser to support Oregon Food Bank and its work to eliminate hunger and its root causes.

waterfrontbluesfest.com

The Bite of Oregon

The Premier Showcase of Brand Oregon Food and Beverage. A slogan like that sets high expectations, and this event delivers. The Bite of Oregon features fine restaurants from all across Oregon, showcasing the bounty this state has to offer.

biteoforegon.com

Woodburn Tulip Festival: Mid-March – Mid-April

If you are in the area, we invite you to stop by and enjoy the expansive views of our farm and learn about the other crops we grow in our off-season! Taste some estate grown wine and wander through our summer event garden!

woodenshoe.com

Winston Championship Drag Races : Various Schedules

For the sixth consecutive time, Woodburn Dragstrip was honored as the NHRA Track of the Year in Division 6. Based on votes submitted by racers and fans throughout the Northwest, the award was presented at the NHRA Division Awards Banquet held at the Seattle DoubleTree hotel.

woodburndragstrip.com
